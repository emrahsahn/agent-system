# Stakeholder Brief

## Objective

Translate execution details into clear stakeholder communication.

## Structure

- What changed
- Why it matters
- Risk and mitigation
- Timeline and next milestones
- Decisions needed from stakeholders

## Output

One-page brief in plain language with clear asks.
